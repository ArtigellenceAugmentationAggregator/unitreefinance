§ 06 · Compliance & Discipline · Non-Negotiable For A Broker

Your name stays clean. Always.

You're a commercial finance broker under an Australian Credit Licence. Every piece of content, every outbound email, every voice-agent transcript has to sit inside ASIC, ACL and Spam Act rules — even the ones you'd never think of. This is the discipline I hold, not you.

ASIC / ACL discipline

Every piece of published content carries a "general information only, not personal financial or credit advice" disclaimer. Your ACL number appears in every email footer. The Voice Agent never quotes rates, never confirms approval, never gives credit advice — it captures, qualifies, books, hands off. You approve every scope change.

Spam Act 2003

Every outbound email carries identification, consent basis, and a working unsubscribe. The 900-contact list gets a "still want to hear from us?" re-permission pass before any marketing sends. unitreefinance.online is used only for compliant outreach — your main unitreefinance.com.au address is never put at risk. Penalties for getting this wrong run to six figures a day. We simply never risk it.

MFAA / FBAA display & credentials

Your industry-body membership (MFAA or FBAA), ACL number, and complaint-handling info displayed correctly across the site, GBP, LinkedIn — Findable Layer includes this as trust-signal metadata. If any credential is missing or out-of-date, I flag it in the Phase 0 Findable Diagnostic.
